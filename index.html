RESEARCH
My current research relates to the FCC Incentive Auction , that consisted of a descending clock reverse auction designed to procure broadcasting rights from TV stations. I am currently working on developing a generative model for constraint graphs that can be used for simulating reverse auctions in different spectrum auction settings.
